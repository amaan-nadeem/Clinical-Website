Accessibility through keyboard

  • You can Easily navigate to each page and section this website contains through keyboard.
  • By Pressing Tab, you can navigate to each link of the navigation bar and by pressing Enter the page will automatically go on to that link.
  • Shift + Tab used to go in reverse or to the previous link like if you are on the services link and you want to be on the home link you can press Shift + Tab to go back to the previous link.
  • To Scroll on to the page you are at use up and down arrow to perform this task.
